A Story Born in Naples

Mamma Mia Ristorante was born in the heart of Naples in 1978, when Nonna Lucia Ferrante first opened her kitchen doors to the neighborhood.

Armed with recipes passed down through four generations and an unshakeable belief that food is love made edible, she created a place where strangers became family.

Today, her grandchildren carry that same spirit — handcrafting every pasta, simmering every sauce, and welcoming every guest as one of their own.

La Nostra Storia

Born in the Heart of Naples

Mamma Mia Ristorante was founded in Naples, Italy in 1978 by Nonna Lucia Ferrante — a woman who believed, above all else, that food is the language of love. Her small trattoria on Via Ferrante quickly became the neighborhood's living room, a place where the smell of simmering ragù drifted through open windows and laughter never stopped.

In 1994, her son Marco carried the family's recipes and spirit to a new home in Jakarta, Indonesia — bringing the warmth of a Neapolitan kitchen to a city hungry for something real. The transition was not easy, but the food spoke for itself. Within a year, regulars were coming nightly, and the waiting list stretched to weeks.

Today, Lucia's grandchildren — Sofia and Luca — run the restaurant with the same fierce devotion. Every pasta is hand-rolled the way their grandmother taught them. Every sauce is simmered low and slow. Every guest is welcomed like family, because at Mamma Mia, that is exactly what they are.

Over forty years of stories, flavors, and faces — and we are only just getting started.

Meet the Family

The hearts behind every dish

Nonna Lucia Ferrante

Founder & Original Chef

The woman who started it all. Lucia opened her kitchen to Naples in 1978 with nothing but family recipes, a wood-fired oven, and a heart full of love. Her spirit lives in every dish we serve.

Marco Ferrante

Second Generation · Executive Chef

Lucia's son, who brought the family tradition to Indonesia in 1994. Marco spent twelve years refining his craft in Rome and Florence before returning to carry his mother's legacy abroad.

Sofia & Luca Ferrante

Third Generation · Co-Directors

Lucia's grandchildren now helm the restaurant with the same passion and precision. Sofia leads the kitchen, Luca manages the floor — together, they ensure every guest leaves feeling like family.
